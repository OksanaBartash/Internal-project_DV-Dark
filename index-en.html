GOOD EVENING - WE ARE FROM UKRAINE

The support program for Ukrainians affected by the consequences of Russia's war against Ukraine and the Ukrainian people.

Questionnaire of the program participant

Preferred field of study

We are creating and continually developing a global network of offline/online support centers for Ukrainians who have been affected by the consequences of Russia's war against Ukraine and the Ukrainian people. Especially for those who have been forced to leave their permanent places of residence.

We provide

Intensive education in IT specialties for refugees/displaced persons

Individual support for each person until guaranteed employment

Guaranteed return and adaptation to their cities and villages in Ukraine after our victory

If you have been affected by the consequences
of the war in Ukraine,
please register to participate
in the program:

We attract financial, material resources, and expertise from countries, foundations, businesses, individuals from abroad to Ukraine, both for direct and indirect support of the program, and thus the Ukrainian army and the Ukrainian people.

The goal is to attract as many resources into Ukraine as possible, rather than extracting them from it. This will kickstart and revitalize the Ukrainian economy, create new jobs for Ukrainians, especially those who have been most affected by Russia's war against Ukraine. All of this will contribute to the defense,rapid development of Ukraine, and the personal well-being of every Ukrainian and their families.
